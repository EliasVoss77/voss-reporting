// How We Calculate

VOSS tracks three distinct financial metrics:

Chat Value =
  Hours Saved × $65/hr × AI Contribution %

Implementation Cost =
  Σ (Hours to Complete × Role Rate × Headcount)
  × Industry Multiplier

Projected Business Value =
  AI estimate of business ROI when tasks complete

Chat Value — what this AI session saved in time and money.

Implementation Cost — realistic labor cost to complete all planned tasks. Adjust role, headcount, and hours per task.

Projected Business Value — estimated economic value the business gains when these tasks are completed. Based on impact type and industry multipliers.

Industry Multiplier — scales implementation cost estimate based on your industry's labor market.
